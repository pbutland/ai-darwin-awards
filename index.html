What Are the AI Darwin Awards?

The AI Darwin Awards honour those who, in their quest for technological glory (or perhaps just a quick buck), have used artificial intelligence in ways so reckless, short-sighted, or downright daft that they threaten the very future of humanity. If you thought the original Darwin Awards were wild, just wait until you see what happens when you add machine learning to the mix!

Nomination Criteria

  • Must involve the use (or misuse) of modern AI technologies.
  • The action must be so ill-conceived that it could plausibly endanger the future of humankind (or at least make us all facepalm in unison).
  • Bonus points for hubris, lack of foresight, or a complete disregard for ethical considerations.

Winning Criteria

  • The nominee's actions must have a demonstrable impact or risk (theoretical or real) to society, safety, or the planet.
  • Extra credit for creative stupidity, viral notoriety, or inspiring a new round of AI safety regulations.
  • Winners are selected by popular vote, expert panel, or the AI itself (what could possibly go wrong?).
