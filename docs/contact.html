Get in Touch

What to Expect

Your message will be read by a real person (revolutionary, we know) who'll actually read your message and attempt to respond in a timely fashion, assuming you're not asking us to debug your personal AI chatbot or explain why your smart toaster has achieved sentience.

We run this website because we find it amusing, not because we want to become a customer service centre for every AI-related question on the internet. We're happy to help with legitimate enquiries, but please note that we're not Customer Service Central for every AI-related question in the universe.
